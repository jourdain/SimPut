Energy

The specification of the form of the energy equation.

form - The form of the energy equation (string, default=isothermal). The form must be one of the following:

  • Isothermal - No energy equation is solved - the flow is isothermal,
  • Temperature - The energy equation is solved in terms of temperature,
  • Enthalpy - The energy equation is solved in terms of specific enthalpy, or
  • Internal Energy - The energy equation is solved in terms of internal energy.
