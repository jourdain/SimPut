Material

A material model contains the necessary material parameters to describe material behavior for a range of physics. Material sets describe what materials appear in various regions of the physical domain, i.e., what parts of the computational mesh are contained in a material set.

name - A descriptive material name (string, default=null). name is not used in the derived control file. A unique id for each specific material is generated underneath the cover of the web interface. While providing a descriptive name is not required, these descriptive names clearly increase usability.

rho - The material mass density, ρref (float, default=1.0).

Cp - The constant-pressure specific heat, Cp (float, default=1.0).

Cv - The constant-pressure specific heat, Cv (float, default=1.0).

k - The symmetric thermal conductivity tensor, k, {k11, k12, k13, k22, k23, k33} (6 x float, default=identity kii = 1.0 or for i≠j kij = 0.0). For fluid dynamics problems, this is the only thermal conductivity that is required.

mu - The molecular viscosity, μ (float, default=1.0).

Tref - The material reference temperature, Tref (float, default=0.0).

beta - The material coefficient of thermal expansion, β (float, default=0.0).

rigid - Rigid body material specification for native conjugate heat transfer, rigid flag (string, default=off). Valid values are the following:

  • off - treat material as a fluid or deformable body, or
  • on - treat material as a rigid body.

vel - The x-, y-, and z-components of the rigid body velocity for native conjugate heat transfer, vel, {velx, vely, velz} (3 x float, default={0.0, 0.0, 0.0}).

blockids - One (or more) element set(s) or block Id(s) for this material set, blockId (integer, required).

type - The type of material set (string, default=Eulerian). The type must be one of the following:

  • Eulerian - Use Eulerian coordinates for this material set,
  • Lagrangian - Use Lagrangian coordinates for this material set, or
  • ALE - Use an arbitrary Lagrangian-Eulerian description for this material set.
