Initial Conditions

For many CFD problems, a simplified specification of initial conditions is sufficient. Hydra-TH provides a simplified input mechanism for prescribing all initial conditions.

tke - The turbulent kinetic energy, k (float, default=0.0) supporting k - ε and k - ω models.

eps - The turbulent dissipation rate, ε (float, default=0.0) with support for k - ε models.

omega - The inverse dissipation time scale, ω (float, default=0.0), which is used for k - ω models.

turbnu - The turbulent viscosity, υ (float, default=0.0) for Spalart-Allmaras and DES models.

temperature - The temperature, T (float, default=0.0) (aka: temp).

enthalpy - The enthalpy, h (float, default=0.0).

int_energy - The internal energy, e (float, default=0.0).
